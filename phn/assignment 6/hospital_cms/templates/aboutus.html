About Evergreen Medical Center

Our History

Evergreen Medical Center was founded in 1990 with a vision to provide exceptional healthcare services to the community. Over the years, we have grown into a leading medical institution known for our commitment to quality care, advanced technologies, and compassionate approach.

Achievements and Recognitions

  • Recipient of the Excellence in Healthcare Award
  • Certified as a Center of Excellence for Cardiology Services
  • Accredited by the Joint Commission
  • Recognized as a Top Hospital for Patient Safety

Our Specializations

Cardiology

Our experienced cardiologists provide comprehensive care for heart-related conditions, including diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing management. We utilize advanced cardiac technologies to deliver the highest standard of care.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic specialists offer a range of services for musculoskeletal conditions, from joint replacements to sports medicine. We strive to enhance mobility and improve quality of life for our patients.

Oncology

Our oncology team provides personalized cancer care, including diagnosis, treatment planning, and advanced therapies. We are dedicated to supporting patients throughout their journey and achieving the best possible outcomes.

Neurology

Our neurologists specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders, such as stroke, epilepsy, and multiple sclerosis. We offer advanced diagnostic techniques and innovative treatments to optimize patient care.
